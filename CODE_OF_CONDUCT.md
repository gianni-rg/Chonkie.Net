# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inspiring community for all. We expect all participants in the Chonkie.Net community to uphold this code of conduct both online and during in-person community events hosted or affiliated with Chonkie.Net.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

* Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Giving and gracefully accepting constructive criticism
* Focusing on what is best for the community
* Showing empathy towards other community members
* Being patient and understanding with others
* Helping to resolve conflicts in a constructive manner

Examples of unacceptable behavior by participants include:

* The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
* Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
* Public or private harassment
* Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
* Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
* Discrimination based on race, ethnicity, gender, gender identity and expression, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, religion, or any other characteristic
* Intimidation or threats of violence

## Enforcement

Community leaders who do not follow or enforce this Code of Conduct in good faith may face temporary or permanent repercussions as determined by other members of the project's leadership.

### Reporting a Violation

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

When reporting, please provide:

* Your contact information
* Names (real, nicknames, or pseudonyms) of any individuals involved
* Your account/profile information if applicable
* Description of the incident
* Where the incident occurred
* Any additional context

All reports will be handled with discretion. The identity of the person filing a report will be kept confidential when requested.

### Consequences

Depending on the severity and nature of the violation:

1. **Warning**: A private message clarifying the expectations and the impact of their behavior. A public apology may be requested.
2. **Temporary Ban**: A temporary exclusion from community spaces (issue discussions, pull requests, etc.) for a specified period.
3. **Permanent Ban**: Indefinite exclusion from community spaces.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

* All community spaces online (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, etc.)
* All in-person community events affiliated with or representing Chonkie.Net
* Any communication channel used by the community

This Code of Conduct applies equally to all community members, including maintainers and contributors.
